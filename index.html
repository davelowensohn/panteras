¡Panteras Fuertes!

The current structure and policy for school fundraising in PPS has major problems in terms of equity, with major disparities in how supplemental funding is distributed across the district. At Rigler, we are committed to both ensuring that our kids have the best possible educational and social experience AND working to create greater equity in our schools. Here's how you can help.

  • Donate
  • Get Involved

Why do we need your support?

Rigler Elementary in NE Portland is a Title 1 school serving the most racially and ethnically diverse zip code in Oregon. Due to a combination of declining enrollment and the rise of privately funded, school-specific foundations, schools like ours are at a disadvantage when it comes to access to supplemental funding. We believe equitable fundraising should be organized district-wide, which is why we do not participate in the current Portland Public Schools Foundation system.

FAQ

Is my donation tax deductible?
Yes. Padres Unidos, officially registered as Rigler Elementary PTA, is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, and 100% of your donation is tax deductible. Our EIN is 94-3039328.

I thought local school foundations (LSFs) were a good thing?!
There's a lot to say here, but in brief: they are if your students are wealthy, less so if they aren't. For a lot more nuance and exploration, we recommend the following resources:

  • Reform PPS
  • "A Years-Long Rift Over School Fundraising Comes To A Head In Portland" @ Portland Monthly, November 2022

What is Padres Unidos?
Padres Unidos, also known as the Rigler Elementary PTA, is a parent-led organization providing social and academic support for Rigler Elementary students, families and teachers. Funds are used for community building, cultural and educational events and programs, and to provide grants for classroom projects and supplies.
